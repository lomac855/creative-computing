CONDITIONAL DESIGN

How It Works

3 players are each assigned a different shape (squares, lines and circles or arrows).

1. During each turn (lasting only 10 sec) a player begins to draw a part of an animal. Only one part must be drawn: i.e. ears, nose or leg.
2. The next player must add on by then drawing another body part of the animal they think is being drawn. Must be a part of the animal but does not have to link to the previous part drawn.
3. Repeat until a fully formed animal can be seen.

First Attempt

I gave the players too much time to complete their part which led to one of them drawing a fully formed figure within the 30 seconds so I modified it to 10.
