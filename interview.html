A BODY OF KNOWLEDGE

An Interview w/ Cole Masaki: on Digital Design

What is Digital Design to You?
Creating/designing on an digital platform.

How Did You Start?
Well, when I was four I used to use MS Paint with my mum everyday and I loved drawing on the computer and when I was nine I really wanted to make action movies so I would take frames from iMovie and add effects to them on Photoshop . That's when I really began to explore Adobe After Effects and from there I have had a passion for learning new programmes and all their capabilities.

What's Your Favourite Thing to Create?
I really enjoy all kinds of 2D and 3D work but I tend to gravitate towards projects that are more surrealistic.

Where do You Find Inspiration?
I like to think that I draw inspiration from everywhere. Any kind of media or object can be the inspiration for a project and I don't want to limit myself to any one source of inspiration.
